Dagim Sisay

Coder

Dagim fell in love with computers and programming at a very early age in his life. Over the course of the years, he developed his skills on several programming languages such as python, Java, C/C++, and PHP with which he has worked on many passion projects. Some of his work include a Verilog dump file parser, speech gender classification and Amharic text to speech. In addition to his skills as a programmer, Dagim is also known to dabble with graphics design. This enables him to not only make systems that work, but those that look good while doing so.

Kidus Wendimagegn

Project Manager

Kidus’s early experience with team work had always been horrible. He was often left to do all the work by himself which was often strenuous and inefficient. This made him truly appreciate the value of division of labor. So, with this inspiration, he set out to develop his own skills in communication and management. He was able to join student clubs such as Model United Nations where he spent 4 years serving as a delegate and a chairperson, AIESEC in AAU where he was a project manager for a program called Y.E.S 2.0 (Young Entrepreneur Students) and Toastmasters. These helped him develop his presentation, communication, crises management and timemanagement skills. He then went on his internship period serving as a team leader for the software projects he was working on.
